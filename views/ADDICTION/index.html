About Addiction 2022

The "Main event" - 19 April, 2022

19th of April 2022 marked as the first day of the three-day cultural event which was held at PICT campus and inaugurated by Hon’ble Principal Mam Dr. R. Sreemathy. The theme for the day was Famous Personalities and India’s Greatest Epics. The students took charge and started with ‘Dhol-Tasha’ performance. Students and staff came up in the attire of different famous personalities. It was really difficult to pick winners as all were unique in their own way. Everyone enjoyed some of the side events and the various performances. The anchors did a great job in entertaining the audience as well. The audition team, coordination team, VNL team, design team and photography team all did a great job backstage to bring the dances, songs and acts to life on stage.

The theme day - 20 April, 2022

20th of April 2022 was the second day of the event. The theme for the day was Unity in Diversity & Indian Diverse Culture. The whole campus turned into a collection of traditions from all over the country. The teachers also had a blast reliving our traditions and the students’ performances. DJ concluded the second day.

The Contradiction & Masquerade day - 21 April, 2022

21st of April 2022 was the last day of the event and was celebrated as Contradiction & Masquerade Day. The BE students took charge and infused whole atmosphere with energy through the amazing performance The event came to a rocking end by the beats of the DJ which turned the campus into a dance floor. The event left all with loads of joy and memories for a lifetime.

About Rangavarsha 2020

Rangavarsha started in the year 14-15, Jan 15 2015 was first time when rangavarsha was celebrated.

On the auspicious day of 15th Jan, 2020, Rangavarsha was celebrated by our very talented staff of all branches in PICT. On this day we presented events like drama, singing, one act play, dancing etc. that is all form of arts.
