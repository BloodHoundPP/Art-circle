Clubs

OUR DIFFERENT CLUBS

Extra Curricular Activities (ECA) committee of PICT is a union of three large scale clubs, which are the PICT Art Circle, PICT DEBSOC, and PICT Quiz Club. Together, the committee focuses on the extracurricular development of the students of PICT, by making them find their hidden talents, and helping them showcase the same. Each club focuses on a unique development curriculum, which is discussed below:

  • PICT DEBSOC:Established in March 2019, the club focuses on developing the debating and the oratory skills of its members. The club focuses on developing the spontaneity in thinking and the way in which our thoughts should be put forth. Along with that, the club encourages its members to take part in debating competitions, and to display their oratory skills at the higher level.
  • PICT Art Circle: The club focuses on bringing out the artistic side of the students, by encouraging them to participate in theatrical performances like Firodiya Karandak and Purushottam Karandak. Along with that, the club provides a platform to showcase their dancing and singing skills. The club has officially represented PICT at many competitions, and has a brilliant record of performing excellently at many prestigious platforms.
  • PICT Quiz Club: The club is a group of like-minded enthusiasts who organize quiz competitions at the college level. The club also represents the college at different quizzing events.
